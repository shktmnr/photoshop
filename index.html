Introduction

This 6-week Photoshop training program equips students with essential skills like photo editing, image manipulation, and digital design using Adobe Photoshop. The course covers layers, masks, color correction, and more, while encouraging students to explore additional features like filters, blending modes, and 3D effects on their own. By combining structured lessons with independent exploration, students will develop a deeper mastery of the software. The program includes hands-on projects, and concludes with a quiz test and certification, ensuring participants are ready to apply their skills professionally..

Course Duration: 6 weeks, 6 classes per week, 2 hours per class

Target Audience: Beginners to Intermediate Learners
