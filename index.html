Adobe Photoshop Module

Complete Guide to Mastering Photoshop - Covering All Tools

Introduction

This module provides a comprehensive training in Adobe Photoshop. Over the next 4 weeks, students will learn all the tools in Photoshop, from basic to advanced techniques, and apply their skills to real-world projects.

Course Duration: 4 weeks, 6 classes per week, 2 hours per class

Target Audience: Beginners to Intermediate Learners
