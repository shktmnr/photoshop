Course Introduction

Welcome to the Adobe Photoshop module at Basics International Software and Cyber Zone! This comprehensive 4-week course is designed for beginners to intermediate learners who wish to master Adobe Photoshop. Over the course of four weeks, students will gain hands-on experience with all tools, features, and techniques necessary to create stunning graphics and photo edits.

Weekly Breakdown

Day Topics
Day 1 Overview of Adobe Photoshop, Interface Tour, Setting Up Your Workspace
Day 2 Understanding File Formats, Opening and Saving Files
Day 3 Introduction to Selection Tools, Rectangular Marquee Tool, Lasso Tool
Day 4 Quick Selection Tool, Magic Wand Tool, Refining Selections
Day 5 Basic Navigation and Zooming Techniques, Using the Rulers and Guides
Day 6 Introduction to Layers, Creating, Deleting, and Managing Layers

Week 2: Working with Text and Shapes

Day Topics
Day 1 Introduction to the Type Tool, Creating and Formatting Text
Day 2 Working with Shape Tools, Creating Custom Shapes
Day 3 Layer Styles and Effects, Applying Styles to Text and Shapes
Day 4 Introduction to the Pen Tool, Creating Paths and Custom Shapes
Day 5 Understanding Layer Masks, Creating and Using Masks
Day 6 Grouping and Flattening Layers, Exporting Images with Text

Week 3: Advanced Editing Techniques

Day Topics
Day 1 Color Correction Techniques, Using Adjustment Layers
Day 2 Retouching Tools Overview, Healing Brush Tool and Clone Stamp Tool
Day 3 Working with Filters, Applying and Customizing Filters
Day 4 Creating and Using Actions, Automating Repetitive Tasks
Day 5 Introduction to Blending Modes, Understanding Layer Interactions
Day 6 Creating a Composite Image, Final Touches on Compositions

Week 4: Project and Review

Day Topics
Day 1 Introduction to Projects, Planning Your Project
Day 2 Working on Individual Projects, Applying Skills Learned
Day 3 Project Work Continues, One-on-One Feedback Sessions
Day 4 Finalizing Projects, Preparing for Presentation
Day 5 Presenting Projects to Class, Group Feedback and Discussion
Day 6 Course Review and Wrap-Up, Q&A Session

Tools Covered

  • Selection Tools: Rectangular Marquee Tool, Lasso Tool, Quick Selection Tool, Magic Wand Tool
  • Editing Tools: Brush Tool, Clone Stamp Tool, Healing Brush Tool, Eraser Tool
  • Text Tools: Type Tool, Character and Paragraph Panels
  • Shape Tools: Rectangle Tool, Ellipse Tool, Custom Shape Tool
  • Adjustment Layers: Brightness/Contrast, Levels, Curves
  • Filters: Gaussian Blur, Sharpen, Distort Filters
  • Advanced Tools: Pen Tool, Layer Masks, Blending Modes
