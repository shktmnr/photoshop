Course Introduction

Learn the industry's leading software for creating stunning visuals. Our course covers essential techniques for image editing, graphic design, and visual storytelling to empower you in today’s visual world.

Prerequisites

  • Basic Computer Knowledge: Familiarity with computers and general software usage.
  • Passion for Creativity: A strong interest in graphic design, art, or visual creation.
  • English Proficiency: Basic understanding of English for interface navigation and terminology.

Course Characteristics

  • Duration: 12 Weeks
  • Frequency: 3 days per week
  • Mode: In-person training
  • Class Size: Limited to ensure individual attention
  • Instructor: Experienced graphic designers with industry knowledge

Learning Modules

  • Introduction to Photoshop: Workspace, tools, and interface basics
  • Working with Layers: Layer management and blending techniques
  • Photo Editing and Retouching: Correction techniques, filters, and effects
  • Creating Digital Art: Vector shapes, illustrations, and digital drawing basics
  • Text and Typography: Advanced text effects, font styling, and formatting
  • Advanced Design Techniques: Smart objects, layer styles, and compositing
  • Projects and Portfolio Building: Real-world projects for a personal portfolio

Benefits of the Course

  • Creative Freedom: Develop skills to bring your ideas to life.
  • Career Opportunities: Preparation for roles like Graphic Designer, Photo Editor, Digital Artist.
  • Hands-on Projects: Complete design projects for your professional portfolio.
  • Industry-Relevant Skills: Expertise in tools and techniques in demand across industries.
  • Global Recognition: Stand out with globally recognized Photoshop skills.

Technical Requirements

  • Laptop/Computer: With adequate processing power
  • Adobe Photoshop: Software version installed (provided by the academy)
  • Basic Knowledge of Image Formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF, etc.
  • Notebook: For taking notes and drafting ideas

Global Opportunities

With Photoshop skills, explore careers in Graphic Design, Digital Marketing, Photography, Video Games, and Freelancing, where your designs can reach global clients.
